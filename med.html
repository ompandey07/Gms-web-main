About

    Master of Education in Nepal is a two years semester based comprehensive program that aims at generating qualified educational professionals such as teachers, trainers, teacher educators, tutors, curriculum specialists, counsellors, educational planners, managers/administrators, education specialists and researchers. Currently, Master of Education (M.Ed) education in Nepal is offered by Tribhuvan University. Kathmandu University, Purbanchal University and Mid Western University. Being an interdisciplinary programme, M.Ed offers a wide range of specializations and majors. If you are interested in pursuing a career in the field of education and want to become an educator you may opt for the M.Ed programme. By pursuing the M.Ed programme, you can make a positive impact on the lives of students and overall changemaker in the field of education. Educational leaders are responsible to promote effective approaches to transform and innovate and change the educational system.

Master of Education (M.Ed) Programs of Tribhuvan University

    Master of Education is a two years program offered by the Faculty of Education in constituent and affiliated campuses/colleges across the country. The program involves professional/pedagogical core courses, specialized courses and elective courses.

Specialization subjects offered in M.Ed Programme:

  • Curriculum and Evaluation
  • Education Planning and Management
  • English Education
  • Nepali Education
  • Science Education
  • Mathematics Education
  • Health Education
  • Physical Education
  • Population Education
  • Economics Education
  • Geography Education
  • History Education
  • Political Science Education

In addition to specializing in any one of these subjects, you are required to conduct independent research work and participate in teaching and related activities (practicum).
