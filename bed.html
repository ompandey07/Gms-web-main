Overview

Tribhuvan University introduced a three year B.Ed program with one specialization area in 1996 with an aim to produce a competent manpower that can function effectively in the education sector. Later in 2072, the three-year program was revised and extended to four year, so was the specialization area. Today students can specialize in two areas. The Bachelor of Education is an ideal course for those willing to make a career in the education sector. The program furnishes students with the skills and knowledge required to serve as a teacher in the primary and secondary level in school. The program consists of 22 subjects including general English, general Nepali, educational psychology, philosophical and sociological foundation of education etc. Students can specialize in one major subject that includes 10 courses. Students can specialize in areas such as political science education, economics education, geography education, population education, health and physical education, science education, etc.

Some of the Objectives of this program are

  • To produce teachers capable to teach at the primary and secondary level
  • To produce teachers who are capable to teach the differently able students
  • To develop leadership qualities
  • To enable students to function as a course designer, education officer etc
  • To enable the students to adapt in the ever-changing societies and provide education accordingly
  • To encourage lifelong research and learning
  • To build a strong foundation for the higher studies

Career Prospect in Nepal:

The program prepares students to work in the field of the education. This degree holder can find jobs in both private and government academic institutions. Apart from working as a teacher, they can serve in education departments, education consultancies and coaching centers. One willing to be self-employed can run the tuition center.

Duration of Course:

B.Ed program offered by the Tribhuvan University takes four years to complete whereas Purbanchal University has a three year B.Ed program. It is an annual system program.

Eligibility

  • Students interested to study the Bachelor of Education should have
  • Completed 12 years of formal education
  • Should have completed the +2 or Proficiency Certificate Level through the boards recognized by TU and PU
  • Applicant from any stream (science, management and humanities) is eligible to apply for the program

Document Checklist

  • Applicant should submit the following documents at the time of admission:
  • Complete and signed admission form along with an application fee
  • Official copy of academic certificates (SLC onwards)
  • Copy of a character certificate issued by the school
  • Copy of a character certificate issued by the College
  • Copy of citizenship card
  • Transfer Certificate
