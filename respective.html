Bachelor of Computer Applications

Posted on: November 21, 2024

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at GMS is designed to shape the next generation of IT professionals. This comprehensive program combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications to prepare students for the dynamic field of computer science and information technology.

Our curriculum is carefully crafted to provide students with a strong foundation in programming, database management, web development, and software engineering. Through hands-on projects and industry-relevant coursework, students develop the skills needed to excel in today's technology-driven world.

Students will learn from experienced faculty members who bring real-world expertise to the classroom. The program emphasizes both individual and team-based projects, preparing graduates for successful careers in various IT sectors.
